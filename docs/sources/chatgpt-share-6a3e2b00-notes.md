## First Problem Formulation

Modern AI systems scale by increasing:

- model parameters;
- training data;
- computational resources.

Vallace asks whether another axis exists:

```text
I = f(C, K)
```

Where:

- `I` is effective intelligence;
- `C` is computational capability;
- `K` is coordination efficiency.

The fictional hypothesis is that increasing `K` may increase `I`, even when
`C` is fixed.
